Hey, my name is Michael, but some of my friends call me mellow. I'm a 3rd-year student at Simon Fraser University majoring in the Interactive Arts and Technology program. My main passions are in game design, such as things like 3D modeling, programming, and User Experience design. The design skills I have acquired over the years are in prototyping and UX/UI. My software toolkit consists of collaborative design tools like Figma, and 3D modeling software such as OnShape, Rhinoceros3D, and Maya. I have coding proficiency in languages such as Java, Processing, and Arduino.
